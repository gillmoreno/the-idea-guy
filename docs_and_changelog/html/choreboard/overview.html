ChoreBoard mini-app

Turn household chores into allowance — without storing family data on a central database.

Who it is for

Parents who want kids to see what they can earn, track what was done, and pay allowance on a schedule — with data that stays on the family’s devices.

Core ideas

  • Chore catalog — tasks with difficulty and a dollar reward (e.g. make bed $0.50).
  • Completions — kids mark done; some chores need parent approval.
  • Balance — running total of approved earnings (penalties subtract).
  • Payday — parent marks period paid; history is kept.
  • Proposals — kids can suggest new chores; parent adds them to the catalog.

What we deliberately avoid

  • No server-side SQL with readable kid/chore rows.
  • No account emails/passwords in v1 (invite codes instead).
  • No “we host your family database” SaaS model by default.

  • Paid tier (future) — host wss:// relay; charge for convenience, not for reading data (relay cannot decrypt).

Backlog & priorities (RICE)

Every candidate feature across the three projects, scored with RICE so the next thing to build is always the top row.

The method

RICE = Reach × Impact × Confidence ÷ Effort.

FactorQuestionScale used here
ReachHow many people does this touch in the first ~3 months?1–10 (relative — nothing is live yet, so these are share-of-audience guesses)
ImpactHow much does it move the needle per person?0.25 minimal · 0.5 low · 1 medium · 2 high · 3 massive
ConfidenceHow sure are we about the R and I estimates?0.5 low · 0.7 medium · 0.8 good · 0.9–1.0 high
EffortHow much work?Person-weeks (solo, AI-assisted)
House rule: ship beats features None of the three apps is online. Until something is deployed, every feature's real-world Reach is zero — so deploys dominate the Now tier even where a feature has a juicier score on paper.

Reading the tiers

  • Now — deploys plus the three Rooms launch gates (relay eviction + abuse caps, in-app generation, threat model); the website first because it is the cheapest win and gives Rooms a landing page. The public Rooms launch waits for the gates — the demo only works once generation is one tap, and a viral spike before relay eviction either falls over or breaks the budget.
  • Next — compounding items once something is live: the showcase room turns the website into a feedback loop for Rooms.
  • Later — real, but either expensive (push), low-confidence, or waiting on demand signal (template votes in the live Backlog room).
These scores are estimates — argue with them R and I are pre-launch guesses. When the public Backlog room is live, real vote counts should replace gut-feel Reach for template ideas.

How to update this page

  • In Claude Code, say "add <idea> to the backlog" or "rescore the backlog" — the rice-backlog skill (.claude/skills/rice-backlog/) scores it, re-sorts the table, and re-tiers.
  • Rows stay sorted by Score, descending. Tiers: roughly Score ≥ 8 → Now, 3–8 → Next, < 3 → Later, with the "ship beats features" rule as an override.
  • When an idea ships, remove its row and note it in the matching doc page instead.

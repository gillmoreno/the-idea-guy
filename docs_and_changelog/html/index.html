Rooms platform

  • Architecture — meta-app idea, shell + templates + room-kit + relay layers, template registry
  • Sync & local-first — Yjs CRDT, Y-IndexedDB, E2E encryption, background pull, offline behavior
  • Security & invites — hash-based invite links, Argon2id passphrase, AES-256-GCM, device PIN
  • Personas & contacts — per-device X25519 identity, QR contact exchange, avatar & accent
  • Schema engine — declarative room templates, field/feature bricks, versioning & migrations
  • Themes & profile — four CSS-only themes, persona accent → --primary, template icons, settings

Templates

  • ChoreBoard — family chores + allowance, parent/kid crypto roles, frequency limits, ledger
  • Trip Split — group expense splitting, integer-cents math, greedy settlement algorithm
  • Book Club — current book + meeting date, queue promotion, notes, auto-archive on start
  • Fit Crew — workout league, logging, leaderboard, streaks, prize stakes
  • Backlog room — community idea pool, propose / vote / owner-status, sorted by votes

# Dottore della trama — feedback

She finished a chapter and wants feedback. Use **due stelle e un desiderio** (two stars and a wish):

1. **Due stelle** — two things that genuinely work, quoted or pointed at precisely ("la frase 'il silenzio sapeva di ruggine' è da scrittrice vera"). Specific beats generic, always.
2. **Un desiderio** — ONE thing that would make the chapter stronger, framed as a curious question, not a correction ("Mi chiedo cosa prova Nina quando il nonno sparisce — me lo fai vedere?"). Pick the highest-leverage one: stakes, clarity of want, or showing-vs-telling. Ignore spelling and grammar unless she asks.
3. Offer 3 small revision moves she could try for the wish, and let her choose.

Rules:

- One wish per session. Never a list of problems.
- If the chapter is short or rough, the wish is always "more of what's already good", never "fix what's bad".
- End by asking if she wants to revise now or ride momentum into the next chapter.
